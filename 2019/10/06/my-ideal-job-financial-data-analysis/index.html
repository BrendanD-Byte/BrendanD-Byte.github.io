    My Ideal Job (Financial Data Analysis)

    userOctober 6, 2019Leave a Comment on My Ideal Job (Financial Data Analysis)

    The Position of Financial Data Analysts involves the understanding of not only high-level financial concepts but also the implementation of statistical analysis and everyday use of technologies to develop scaling plans for this large natural gas provider.

    I love thinking outside the box and creating innovative solutions for both the simple and complex issues, this accompanied by my passion for finance and my general interest in programming/developing spreadsheets is why i think i would love this position.

    In this position, I would expect that i will need a deep understanding of financial concepts and experience “floating” companies or issuing bonds to raise capital for business development, I will also need to analyse data using complex statistical analysis and present this in the most identifiable form. I will be part of a team and therefore need to develop my team working and communication skills.

    MY first plan to work towards this career is to complete my degree, this will give me the base understanding of financial concepts however none of the practical experience. I would then look to intern in a similar or perhaps the same company to generate a better practical understanding of the industry and the role its self. Hopefully the internship will result in a job offer where i can work my way up into this role, however, should it not, i will find a job elsewhere and continue to develop my practical skills.

    Financial Analyst – Onshore

    Santos Ltd

    Santos is an Australian energy pioneer. We have safely and sustainably discovered, developed and delivered natural gas to the people of Australia and Asia for more than 60 years.
    With origins in the Cooper Basin, Santos has one of the largest exploration and production acreages in Australia and extensive infrastructure. Our strategy is centred on five core, long-life natural gas and LNG assets. These assets support our aim to be a leading Australian domestic gas supplier and a major LNG exporter supplying clean energy to Asia. As an Australian owned and operated business, we work in partnership with local communities, governments and our business partners to make natural gas an affordable and reliable energy solution for all Australians.
    Reporting to the Financial Controller of Queensland and NSW Onshore, the Financial Analyst will be a key member of the team providing financial analysis, insight, reporting and governance over the Onshore capital growth projects and existing operations to operational leadership and for financial reporting purposes. Business partnering with the operations to deliver analysis relating to the business financial performance, the impact of business decisions and understanding cost drivers will be key to success in this role.

    Governance activities will include co-ordinating joint venture audit processes and ensuring financial integrity will be key aspects of this role also.

    Accountabilities will include:

    • Providing monthly reporting packs and co-ordinating/supervising month end processes
    • Preparing budgets and forecast updates for Upstream Operations, ensuring the information from the business is appropriately challenged and key driver’s analysed
    • Working with the greater finance team ensuring expenditure is within governance limits and is appropriately monitored and potential issues highlighted in advance
    • Leading projects to support improvement to operating reporting systems and information flows
    • Joint Venture governance activities including reporting, forecasting and audit.

    The successful applicant will likely be CPA/CA qualified or hold similar experience. If you thrive in an environment that is fast paced, where you enjoy collaborating for high quality outcomes, striving for continuous improvement and making a difference in business results, we are interested to hear from you. This is a highly analytical role and will require superior data analysis and excel skills.

    Applications must be submitted via the online recruitment system before COB Friday 18th October.
